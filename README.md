# customer-segmentation-python
Performed customer segmentation on 793 retail customers 
using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model — 
a real-world marketing analytics technique used by 
companies like Amazon and Netflix.

Tools: Python | Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn

Key Findings:
- Segmented 793 customers into 5 groups — Champions, 
  Loyal, Potential Loyalists, At Risk and Lost
- Champions (25% of customers) contribute 25.3% 
  of total revenue
- Identified 140 At Risk customers for immediate 
  win-back campaigns
- Exported actionable customer segment data for 
  marketing team use

Business Recommendations delivered:
✔ VIP loyalty program for Champion customers
✔ Automated win-back campaigns for At Risk segment
✔ Personalized upsell campaigns for Loyal Customers
✔ Bundle offers for Potential Loyalists
